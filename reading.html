reading.

"A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies. The man who never reads lives only one." — George R.R. Martin

I have always been a voracious reader. It is how I make sense of the world beyond economics. I gravitate towards non-fiction, economic history, and behavioural psychology, but I also love a good novel that pulls me into an entirely different world. I read across languages and genres: anything from neuroscience and philosophy to translated literary fiction. A few of these books have genuinely changed the way I think; the rest have made long train rides between Zürich and Geneva considerably more enjoyable.
